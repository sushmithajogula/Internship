Question : How do I create an account in Multimedia?

Answer : To create an account in Multimedia, enter all the details in the SIGN-UP page by clicking on the link that will appear on the website page. Your account will be created.


Question : How do I log into my Multimedia account?

Answer : Enter your username and password in the LOGIN page and tap Login! button.You will be logged into your Multimedia account.


Question : I can't log in. Why?

Answer : You can here try a few things.

  • Reset your password.
  • Enter your username and password again.


Question : How can I make my password strong?

Answer :

  • Make sure your password is atleast 6 characters long eith a complex combination of numbers, letters and punctuation marks.
  • Try mixing together uppercase and lowercase letters.
  • Keep in mind that your Multimedia account password must be different from the passwords you use to log into other accounts, like your email or bank account.

Multimedia allows users to post and share multimedia content with others. It allows you to upload your photos, documents, files and audio to the website that can be accessed from anywhere in the world. You can then share that media with the world or just a select group of friends. Multimedia allows you to upload your images in high quality, where they can be viewed by other people. Multimedia also allows you to license out your photos under certain common usage licenses – this means you can let other people use your images, for example in their website, so long as they attribute it back to you and abide by certain content restrictions. Multimedia sharing encompasses photo sharing, document sharing, and music sharing. Multimedia strives to make sharing of multimedia easy and fun.
